Talbeena

Talbeena is a soup made from barley flour, formed by adding milk and honey to the dried barley powder. It is called talbeena, which comes from the Arabic word laban meaning yogurt (milk/fermented churned milk), because of its resemblance to yogurt, as it is soft and white. We have different flavours (Chocolate, Banana, Stawberry, Mango, Vanilla, Elaichi, Badam, Pista, SugerFree and others) .
